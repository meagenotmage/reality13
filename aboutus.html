About Us

What is LINK.exe

LINK.exe is a special interest group for WVSU CICT students, dedicated to bridging theory and practice in ICT. It fosters a collaborative community of creative, tech-savvy students exploring the potential of ICT across diverse fields.

Our Core Values and Four Pillars

Enrich

Pillar for Trainings

Spearheads training and workshop, and other such activities that shall enrich the skills of LINK.exe members.

Uphold

The Pillar of Program

Spearheads inter-organizational and/or inter-school programs and activities such as conferences, symposia, contests, and exhibits.

Generate

The Pillar of Income Generate Project

Provides a unique opportunity for members to take the helm of projects, gaining invaluable experience in real-world applications of freelancing through effective team collaboration.

Serve

The Pillar of Extension

Spearheads meaningful activities designed to promote ICT for the benefit of the community, through digital literacy, provide access to technology, and support community development through ICT.
