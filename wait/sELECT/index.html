About sELECT

We, the BSCS 2-A AI team, are developing sELECT (Software for Evaluation and Election) as part of our Software Engineering subject. Our mission is to revolutionize the student voting experience through an efficient, mobile-responsive, and user-friendly platform.

Important: While inspired by the QUESTER platform used in the WVSU student elections, sELECT is not intended to replace QUESTER. It is an academic project created to explore enhancements and address challenges students may have experienced during online voting.
